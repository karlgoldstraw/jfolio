Hi, my name is Karl Goldstraw. I’m a senior interaction / UX designer based near London UK with a passion for accessibility and user centred design.

I currently work for NHS Digital, as a Senior Interaction Designer, which actually spans service design, research, front end and content. I’m happy enough facilitating workshops including design sprints, working with user researchers and building prototypes in HTML and CSS. I'm comfortable working in an agile environment.

I have a particular interest in accessibility, and I’ve done multiple in-house audits for teams over the years. I enjoy advocating best practice as well as finding new ways to help others build empathy. I’m an avid member of our ability network and accessibility working group.
